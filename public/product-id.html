ProSafe 2+

The ProSafe 2+ is a single use disposable suit.

Product Details

Microporous film laminate coverall with three-piece hood and elastication to wrists, waist and ankles. Zipper with double storm flap, three-piece diamond gusset and antistatic to EN1149-1. Sewn and taped seam construction to give spray tight protection.

Features

  • Microporous film laminate
  • Zipper with double storm flap
  • White with red taped seams
  • Sizes M-3XL
  • 50 pieces per carton

Can be used in a range of area:

  • Contact with agrarian chemicals
  • Disposal of asbestos
  • Automotive industry
  • Chemical industry
  • Clean room applications
  • Clean room applications
  • Waste disposal
  • Electronic
  • Emergency teams
  • Food Industry
